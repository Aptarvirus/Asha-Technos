Deepak Gupta professior of IIT KANPUR
Prof. Deepak Gupta is a Professor (HAG) in the Department of Materials Science and Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kanpur, where he currently holds the position of C. D. Gangwal Chair Professor.  His primary research expertise lies in organic electronics, specifically focusing on OLED/PLED displays, thin-film transistors (TFTs), oxide and transparent semiconductors, and the modeling and simulation of materials and processes. 

Prof. Gupta earned his Ph.D.  in Materials Science and Mineral Engineering from the University of California, Berkeley in 1993, following his M.S. from the University of Florida and B.Tech.  from IIT Kanpur. Prior to his academic career, he worked as a Senior Process Engineer at Motorola and as a Post-Doctoral Fellow at Argonne National Laboratory.  He is the Coordinator of the Samtel Center for Display Technologies and has been recognized with honors such as the DST Young Scientist award and the AICTE Career Award for Young Teachers.
